Arbitraging Prediction Markets

Prediction markets are where contract prices reflect collective probability in real time. The bot connects to Polymarket and Kalshi APIs, pulls order-book data, normalizes prices, and compares both markets to detect arbitrage opportunities as soon as they appear.
